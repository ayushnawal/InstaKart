OUR STORY

InternKart is a matchmaking platform which connects experienced professors with bright research aspirants for Research Internships and Research Assistantships abroad. InternKart’s smart algorithm assesses the interest of a student and navigates its rich database to identify suitable research openings around the world

OUR FOUNDERS

Extensive experience in building products for students and universities

Karan Dua

CEO

Karanjeet graduated from the Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee with a Bachelor (Technology) in Biotechnology. During his time as an undergrad, he successfully completed a research project at Duke and Purdue University each. While his strong inclination towards research helped him bag these rewarding opportunities, most of his friends were not so fortunate. Institutionalized research programs such as DAAD and Mitacs are competitive and are usually bagged by students in the Top 1% bracket. The other 99% spend months cold e-mailing professors for a suitable internship position, often without luck. With InternKart, Karan envisioned a smoother application process with a supremely high conversion rate to help his juniors bag research internships. Buoyed by the overwhelming response, InternKart has grown into a full-blown company
